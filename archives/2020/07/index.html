Biography

I'm a Ph. D. student in School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University(SDU), Qingdao, supervised by Prof. Dani Lischinski and Prof. Lin Lu. I got my bachelor degree from Shandong University in 2017.

My research interest is focused on Computer Graphics and Digital Fabrication. Recently, I'm working on structural modeling with microstructures.
